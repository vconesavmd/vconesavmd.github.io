VECTOR (ILLUSTRATOR)

This project was inspired by one of the toys of my two daughters. I chose this character because I wanted to use it in the future animation project.

I took a picture of the real toy and I used it as a template in the background. I used the pen tool to draw all the different parts of the character. I used gradients in an attempt to give some volume to the head and a custom pattern for the hat.
