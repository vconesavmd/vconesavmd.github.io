Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 300gr of Spaghetti pasta
  • 100gr aprox. Bacon or Pancetta cut in cubes
  • 200ml Whipped cream
  • Manchego cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Take a pot and boil the pasta for 8-10 minutes
  2. Take a pan and sprinkle it with a little bit of olive oil.
  3. Half fry the bacon/pancetta.
  4. Lower the heat of the pan to the minimum and pour the whipped cream
  5. Cut the Manchego cheese in fine slices and put them in the pan
  6. Add some salt and pepper
  7. Stir until everything is nicely mixed up
  8. Use a strainer with the cooked pasta
  9. Put the pasta in the pan and mixed everything together
