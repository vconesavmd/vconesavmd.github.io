RASTER (ADOBE PHOTOSHOP)

For this project I was inspired by the recruting posters of World War 1. I wanted to add a funny twist to it and I themed it as Star Wars. I used a special brush for the smoke and a texture to make it look like an old poster.

I researched old posters and I was able to guess the font of one of them using a mobile app called What The Font.
